WELCOME TO PIZZA CO.!

The best pizza you'll ever have, fresh never frozen very cheesy

Pizza Co. was founded in 1973 in the heart of Naples, Italy—the birthplace of pizza—by brothers Marco and Luca Bellini. With a family recipe passed down through generations and a dream of sharing authentic Neapolitan pizza with the world, the Bellinis opened a small pizzeria nestled between the cobbled streets and bustling markets of their hometown. Using only locally sourced San Marzano tomatoes, hand-stretched dough, and wood-fired ovens, they crafted each pie with precision and passion. Word of their mouthwatering margaritas and crispy crusts spread quickly, turning a humble neighborhood spot into a beloved destination for locals and tourists alike.
